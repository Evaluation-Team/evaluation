Evaluating Health Products

Evaluation is the collection of information or data to help us judge the value of a product.

It’s important to conduct an evaluation for all digital health interventions. Doing an evaluation will help you demonstrate your intervention’s:

  • impact on health outcomes
  • cost-effectiveness
  • benefit to the public’s health

Evaluation is much easier if you consider it during the development of your product.

We've created these resources so that you can carry out evaluation in your delivery team, regardless of previous experience and skills.
