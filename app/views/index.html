Evaluating Digital Health Interventions

What is evaluation?

Evaluation is the collection of information or data to help us judge the value of a product or service. .

What is a digital health intervention?

Digital health interventions are products or services that use technology to influence health outcomes. Examples are:

  • weight management apps
  • stop-smoking text messaging services
  • websites that offer tools and techniques to support positive mental health

Why evaluate?

It’s important to conduct an evaluation for all digital health interventions. Doing an evaluation will help you demonstrate your intervention’s:

  • effect on health outcomes
  • cost-effectiveness
  • benefit to the public’s health

This is much easier if you consider evaluation before you develop your intervention.

Case study: Couch to 5K

The Couch to 5K smartphone app was designed to help inactive people develop the confidence and ability to go for a 5 kilometre run.

This process helped the Couch to 5K team design an evaluation strategy for the app – and by following their journey at each step, you’ll see exactly how they did it.
